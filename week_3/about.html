Our Mission

Monroeu is a learning project focused on building accessible and semantic web pages.

We emphasize clean HTML5 markup, proper use of headings, and semantic sections to improve readability and accessibility.

Key Principles

Semantic markup makes content easier to understand for both users and machines.

  • Use clear headings
  • Group related content
  • Provide meaningful alt text
  • Make forms accessible
  • Keep structure consistent
